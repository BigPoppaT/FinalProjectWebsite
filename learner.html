Learner Type

Whats My learner Type?

Our teacher introduced us to the three types of learners: visual, auditory, and kinesthetic/tactile. For an assignment, we had to play three unfamiliar games—Scrabble, Jigsaw Puzzle, and Mahjong—and learn them independently. This activity revealed our learning preferences. After completing the games, our teacher quizzed us to determine our learning styles. Turns out, I'm a kinesthetic/tactile learner, which means I prefer hands-on practice to grasp concepts.
