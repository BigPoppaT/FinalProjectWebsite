Punishment On Teen Killers

Teen Killers and their Punishments

Our teacher assigned us an assignment about teen killers and their punishments. I read an article about a teenager who killed an innocent young girl just for the "thrill of killing someone." The article discussed the punishment he should receive and explained the whole situation. It's a pretty heavy topic to think about.

Rhetorical Devices

Ethos (paragraph 2) - "Charles Stimson, a leading expert in criminal law at Heritage Foundations." - This is Ethos becuase he establishing authority and credibility.

Euphemism (paragraph 5) - "people of nothern Canada had no juvenlile crime at all until 1980 and the introduction of television into their culture" - The author easily couldve said that television brain washes kids into doing violent acts.

Parallelism (paragraph 1) - "...crime even if we do nothing to deter them, while others will seek it out even if we do everthing to reform them....Wicked people exist....except to set them apart from innocent people." - This shows parallelism by using two or more phrases or clauses in a sentence that have the same grammatical structure.
