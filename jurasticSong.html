LAUSD Jurassic 5

Summary

Our teacher gave us this assignment, it was all about this song called "Jurassic 5." The deal was to dig into the lyrics and spot 20 different rhetorical devices in there. Honestly, I wasn't super into the whole rhetorical devices thing before, but this task was a game-changer. It seriously helped me get a grip on what these devices are all about and how to find them not just in songs but in all sorts of texts.

Rhetorical Devices

Alliteration (paragraph 1) - "Dont judge us by bank accounts and big cars. No matter how bright we shine we're far from being stars." - This is Alliteration because it repeats the initial constant letters (or sounds).

Pathos (paragraph 2) - "Where hopes are blown, not even money for the phone. Now tell me what's the solution, how to get back home?" - This is pathos because the writer tries to invoke pity and sadness with that statement.
